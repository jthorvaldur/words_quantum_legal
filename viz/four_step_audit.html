THE FOUR-STEP AUDIT

STRUCTURAL ANALYSIS OF ANY DOCUMENT — PARSE · SCAN · ANALYZE · MAP
1
PARSE EVERY WORD
Decompose each word into prefix | root | suffix. Identify VCC negation operators.
2
SCAN TYPOGRAPHY
Detect DOG-LATIN (GLOSSA), jurisdiction mixing, and case-form classification.
3
ANALYZE SENTENCE STRUCTURE
Parse-syntax compliance per C.S.S.C.P.S.G.P. rules. Detect null chains.
4
MAP JURISDICTION
Determine which jurisdictional layer(s) the document operates in.
