PRACTICAL NOTES

NOTE: Courts expect standard caption format. The DOG-LATIN in captions (CIRCUIT COURT OF COOK COUNTY) is unavoidable in the current system. Focus corrections on the substantive text — arguments, facts, and prayers for relief.
APPROACH: These corrections demonstrate structural improvement. A filing in full parse-syntax form may be challenged by the court as non-conforming. The practical approach is to maximize noun density, eliminate null chains, and reduce pronoun usage within conventional form.
