## ALLOCATION JUDGMENT

IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED, ADJUDGED AND DECREED as follows:

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual and several promises and
undertakings contained herein and for other good and valuable consideration,
the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged, the parties
agree as follows:

1. The Court hereby finds that it has jurisdiction over the parties and the
   subject matter of this cause.

2. IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Petitioner shall forthwith pay to the
   Respondent the sum of maintenance as determined by this Court.

3. The Court further orders that the marital residence shall be sold and
   the proceeds divided equally between the parties.

4. IT IS FURTHER ORDERED AND ADJUDGED that the minor children shall
   reside primarily with the Respondent, subject to the Petitioner's
   parenting time as set forth herein.

5. The parties shall comply with all provisions of this Order. Any
   violation thereof may result in contempt proceedings.

6. This Court retains jurisdiction over all matters not specifically
   addressed herein.

SO ORDERED.

ENTERED: _________________
Judge, Circuit Court of Cook County
